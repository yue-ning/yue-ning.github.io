I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students with backgrounds in computer science or engineering who are interested in data science and applied machine learning research. The expected starting semester is spring 2019 or fall 2019. If you are seriously interested, please mail your CV, personal statement, unofficial transcripts, and one writing sample (in PDFs) to me: yue.ning@stevens.edu or yning@vt.edu.

About me

My research is driven by the complexities of big data and the growing demand of data interpretation in real world problems. Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Applied machine learning in real world problems such as deep learning, multi-task learning, and multi-instance learning for information extraction and event modeling in social science.
  • Text mining and knowledge discovery such as information reciprocity in heterogeneous media sources and multi-modal data mining.
  • Personalization in recommender systems such as user interactions with online systems and privacy-preserving algorithms in distributed frameworks.

About Stevens

As a private research university located in Hoboken, New Jersey, Stevens Institute of Technology is a rising star in the New York city area. Both the university as a whole and the CS department are undergoing a significant growing period. The 55-acre campus is on the Hudson River across from midtown Manhattan within a few minutes from NYC via public transportation. Stevens' superb location offers excellent opportunities for collaboration with nearby universities and careers in both industry and academia.
